Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Combat Timer ("we", "our", or "the app") is a round timer application built for combat sports training. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

Combat Timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

All data created within the app — including custom workouts, session history, and settings — is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

We do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Custom workout configurations
  • App settings and preferences
  • Your last training session

This data is removed when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Combat Timer does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase
  • Facebook SDK
  • Any advertising networks

5. Audio and Device Permissions

Audio — to play bell sounds and voice cues during training sessions. Audio is played locally using files bundled with the app. No audio data is recorded or transmitted.

Vibration — to provide haptic feedback at phase transitions. This permission does not grant access to any personal data.

Screen wake lock — to keep your screen on during training sessions. This does not access any personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

Combat Timer does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app contains no age-restricted content and is suitable for all ages. Since we collect no personal data from any user, no special handling for children's data is required.

7. Data Security

Since Combat Timer stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers, the security of your data depends on the security of your device. We recommend keeping your device and operating system up to date.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Your Rights

Since Combat Timer does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete on request. All data stored on your device can be removed at any time by uninstalling the app.

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not process any personal data, these rights are inherently protected by the design of our app.
