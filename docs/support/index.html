Frequently Asked Questions

The timer stops when my screen locks

Go to Settings in the app and make sure "Keep screen on" is enabled. This keeps your display active during your entire training session.

I can't hear the bell sound

Check the following: make sure "Timer sounds" is enabled in Settings, check the volume slider in Settings, and make sure your device volume is turned up. Combat Timer plays audio even when your device is set to silent mode.

How do I create a custom workout?

Tap the Workout tab, then tap the + button in the top right corner. Set your rounds, round time, rest time, and optionally a warm-up and cool-down. Tap Save — your workout is now available with one tap.

How do I use a preset?

When creating a new workout, tap the Template button next to the name field. Choose from presets across Boxing, Combat Sports, and Conditioning categories. The preset loads all settings instantly — you can adjust them before saving.

The voice cues are not playing

Go to Settings and make sure "Voice cues" is enabled. Voice cues announce each phase by name — Round 1, Rest, Cool down. They play separately from the bell sound so you can enable one without the other.

How do I skip to the next round?

During an active session, tap the arrow on the right side of the phase name to skip forward. Tap the arrow on the left to go back to the previous phase.

Can I lock the screen during training?

Yes. Go to Settings and enable "Lock on start". Once the timer starts the screen is locked to prevent accidental taps. Hold the screen for 1 second to unlock.
